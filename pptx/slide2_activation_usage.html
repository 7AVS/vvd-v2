Key Insights

  • Highest absolute lift in portfolio (+4.28pp overall, 7.6% relative)
  • But: 95% of VVD cards auto-activate at issuance — campaign targets remaining ~5%
  • Small addressable market: ~51K triggers/month, ~130K unique action clients
  • 6 report groups across 2 waves (day 7 activation + day 15 reminder)
  • Channel: Email primary, Digital Online (DO) fallback for non-email-eligible
  • *NIBT uses $78.21/client (new client value) — arguable for activation vs acquisition
Recommendation: Campaign works well. Consider whether activation nudge creates lasting engagement or just accelerates inevitable activation.

Key Insights

  • Heavily restrictive targeting funnel: must be <60 days since activation, low CC user, high debit, foreign currency
  • Only ~580–720 control clients per cohort — far too small for significance
  • Oct 2025 deep dive recommended 3 changes: (1) expand 61+ day window, (2) increase control to 15%, (3) relax 365-day resting rule
  • Implementation status of these 3 recommendations is unknown
  • Baseline usage at ~33% — organic behavior is high, hard to move
Recommendation: Cannot conclude campaign doesn't work — it's underpowered. Implement the Oct 2025 recommendations before next measurement.
