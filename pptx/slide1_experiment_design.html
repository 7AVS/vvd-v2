No formal orchestration between campaigns. Action = communicated to. Control = organic.
Acquisition
Contextual Notification VCN Monthly Trigger
Monthly screening of all non-VVD-holders through a decision tree. Sends a mobile push notification encouraging card acquisition. Targets clients with active personal banking, active in mobile, high debit usage (>$500/month, >10 txns), and low credit card usage (<10 txns/month). Includes newcomers and students 14+.

Seasonal Acquisition VDA Seasonal Batch
Seasonal campaign deployed ~2x/year during high-engagement periods (summer, holiday). Targets non-VVD-holders with active online banking, high debit usage, low credit card usage, and newcomers. Email capped at 250K, prioritized by tenure ≤8 years. Banner/O&O for tenure up to 25 years.

Activation
Activation Trigger VDT Activity Trigger
Triggered when a VVD card is issued but not activated. First email at day 7 post-issuance, reminder at day 15 if still inactive. Targets all clients (including students 13–17) with non-activated cards. ~95% of VVD cards auto-activate at issuance — this campaign addresses the remaining ~5%.
Channel EM (Email) primary  |  DO (Digital Online) fallback for non-email-eligible on activation wave only

Usage
Usage Trigger VUI Activity Trigger
Triggered for existing cardholders who activated their digital VVD card in mobile within the last 60 days but haven't made a purchase. Must also be an active mobile user (login within 90 days). Additional filters: low credit card user, high debit user, foreign currency >$100 in past 2 years. Very restrictive targeting funnel.

Wallet Provisioning
Tokenization VUT One-time Batch
Single batch deployment targeting existing VVD cardholders to add card to digital wallet. Clients must have VVD transaction history, or be high-debit/low-CC users, or have foreign currency activity. Excludes clients targeted by VUI in last 90 days. Originally designed with 6 segments — 5 were removed during revisions (all student and all in-app segments eliminated).

Add To Wallet VAW Monthly
Monthly campaign targeting existing VVD cardholders to add card to Apple Pay (iOS only). Requires: active VVD user (purchase in last 60 days), activated digital/hybrid card in mobile, never provisioned before, low credit card user (<$100/month or <2 txns), active mobile user (login in last 30 days). Same goal as VUT but fundamentally different design — targeted mobile-engaged iOS audience with monthly refresh vs single email blast to broad audience.
