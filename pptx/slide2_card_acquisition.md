### The Over-Contacting Problem

VCN accounts for 80.5% of all portfolio deployments (15.8M) but produces only 3,591 incremental (12 months).

Diminishing returns are severe:

| Contact # | 1 | 2 | 3 | 5 | 10 |
|-----------|------|------|------|------|------|
| Success Rate | 1.25% | 0.65% | 0.50% | 0.33% | 0.23% |
| Decline from #1 | — | -48% | -60% | -74% | -82% |

Median client receives 7 contacts, P90 = 14. 770K (40%) clients sit in the 11+ contact bucket.

MB (mobile push) is a zero-marginal-cost channel, so the economic waste is muted compared to email. However, brand fatigue and app avoidance risk are unmeasured.

**Recommendation**: Implement contact frequency cap (3-5 contacts max) and/or include a resting period.
