VVD Campaign Portfolio
Measurement period: Feb 2025 – Feb 2026  |  19.5M deployments  |  ~4.3M unique clients
VCN — Contextual Notification
Goal: Acquire new VVD cardholders
Channel: Mobile Banking (passive banner)
Deployment: Monthly batch  |  95/5
Audience: 1.96M clients  |  15.5M deployments
Metric: Card acquisition
VDA — Seasonal Acquisition
Goal: Acquire new VVD cardholders
Channel: Email + In-App / In-App only
Deployment: Seasonal (~2x/year)  |  95/5
Audience: 895K clients  |  1.5M deployments
Metric: Card acquisition
VDT — Activation Trigger
Goal: Activate issued-but-inactive cards (~5% of VVD)
Channel: Email (primary) + Push notification
Deployment: Triggered, 7-day + 14-day reminder  |  90/10
Audience: 130K clients  |  737K deployments
Metric: Card activation
VUI — Usage Trigger
Goal: Increase card transactions
Channel: Email + In-App / Email / In-App
Deployment: Triggered  |  95/5
Audience: 149K clients  |  372K deployments
Metric: Card usage
VUT — Tokenization
Goal: Add card to digital wallet
Channel: Email (single contact)
Deployment: Triggered  |  95/5
Audience: 785K clients  |  826K deployments
Metric: Wallet provisioning
VAW — Add To Wallet
Goal: Add card to digital wallet (Apple Pay)
Channel: In-App message (iOS only)
Deployment: Triggered, single contact  |  80/20
Audience: 402K clients  |  502K deployments
Metric: Wallet provisioning
Control groups are organic (not communicated to)   |   Treatment windows based on deployment dates
