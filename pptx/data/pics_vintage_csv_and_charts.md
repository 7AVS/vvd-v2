### Visual Pattern:
- All curves start near 0% at Day 0 and rise steeply
- By Day 10: curves reach ~30-35%
- By Day 20: curves reach ~40-45%
- By Day 30: curves flatten around 42-50%
- **Action and Control lines are virtually indistinguishable** — they overlap almost perfectly
- One or two dashed cohort pairs (2026-01?, 2026-02?) sit slightly above the main cluster (~48-50% vs ~42-45%)
- Title confirms: "+0.64pp n.s." (not significant — 0.64 percentage point lift, not statistically significant)

### Key Observation:
This is the definitive visual proof that VUI has no effect. The vintage curves for Action and Control are essentially identical across all 13 cohorts.
