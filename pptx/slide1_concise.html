VVD Campaign Measurement — Experiment Design

6 independent A/B tests | Jan 2025 – Feb 2026
Action = communicated to. Control = organic. No orchestration between campaigns.
Acquisition
Contextual Notification VCN Monthly Trigger
Monthly screening of non-VVD-holders via decision tree. Mobile push notification encouraging card acquisition. Targets high-debit, low-credit-card clients active in mobile banking. Includes students 14+.
MB (Mobile Push) | Split 95/5 | Window 30d | 12 cohorts · ~1.1M per cohort | Total: ~2.0M action
Flag: Persistent SRM (94.7/5.3 vs designed 95/5)
Seasonal Acquisition VDA Seasonal Batch
Seasonal campaign (~2x/year) targeting non-VVD-holders active in online banking. Two-arm channel test: Email+Banner (250K cap) vs Banner only (no cap). Tenure-based email prioritization (≤8 years).
EM+IM vs IM only | Split 95/5 | Window 90d | 2 cohorts (Jul, Nov 2025) · ~755K per wave | Total: ~895K action
Flag: SRM observed (92/8 vs designed 95/5)
Activation
Activation Trigger VDT Activity Trigger
Triggered 7 days post-card-issuance + day 15 reminder. Targets the ~5% of VVD cards that don’t auto-activate. Student and non-student segments. DO channel is fallback for non-email-eligible, not a deliberate test.
EM (Email) primary, DO fallback | Split 90/10 | Window 30d | 14 cohorts · ~51K per cohort | Total: ~130K action
Usage
Usage Trigger VUI Activity Trigger
Triggered for cardholders who activated in last 60 days but haven’t transacted. Restrictive funnel: must be low-CC, high-debit, foreign currency user, active mobile. Oct 2025 deep dive recommended expanding population and increasing control — status unknown.
EM+IM / EM / IM (by eligibility) | Split 95/5 | Window 30d | 13 cohorts · ~28K per cohort | Total: ~149K action
Note: Very small control (~600 per cohort)
Wallet Provisioning
Tokenization VUT One-time Batch
Single email blast to existing VVD users to add card to digital wallet. Originally 6 segments — 5 removed (all student + all in-app eliminated). 90-day VUI exclusion resting rule.
EM (Email) only | Split 95/5 | Window 90d | 1 cohort (Jun 2025) | 785K action
Note: Known SAS bug in success definition
Add To Wallet VAW Monthly
Monthly in-app message to iOS users to add VVD to Apple Pay. Targets active VVD users (purchase in 60 days), mobile-engaged, never provisioned. Same goal as VUT but fundamentally different design.
IM (In-app, iOS only) | Split 80/20 | Window 30d | 10 cohorts · ~40K per cohort | Total: ~402K action
  1. Static control groups (VCN, VDT) use client SRF for persistent holdout.
  2. VUI → VUT: 90-day resting rule is the only cross-campaign dependency.
